Art Concept

Procedural palimpsest of traditional and digital art layers.

AI / Machine Learning is quickly growing into a pan-art system, where all possibilities of audio/visual experience are only one compute cycle away from reaching our eyes and ears. This is why I think it's important to build art systems that bring together traditional analog and digital artworks that are tied to their creators. Now that art experiences are happening on platforms like YouTube and Twitch, where the audience can participate via chat, it is possible for the crowd to communally 'play' the very artwork that they created, in real time.

Palimpsest

One major component and theme in my work is palimpsest, which is the complex result of layering information and observing emergent ideas. Future art will be increasingly about combining multiple ideas into one living and changing composite, in real-time.

VR

Not only does VR give us the third dimension in experiencing digital art, but it also makes manipulating a fouth dimension much more accessible. Each of our body's joints being tracked at high frequency as they move through space gives incredible control of many variables in a generative program. Soon we will have VR avatars that are more like musical instruments than costumes. (see below)

Human

The human avatar component in my work is so far the most under-developed, and perhaps the most exciting, as it feels like a perfect opportunity for me to embrace and integrate the mental-awareness components of my life into my work. read: dancing alleviates anxiety.

Curriculum Components

  • Micro Content Output

    Producing daily content is important for growth, momentum, and exposure. My schedule will include outputing a simple/achievable piece of content daily that can be refined into being part of a whole experience (see Macro Infrastructure Development)

  • Tutorials & Documentation

    Producing teaching content is important for community and knowledge capturing. Along with daily content output, I will document useful snippets of my process in video form.

  • Macro Infastructure Development

    I have a vivid vision of the future of procedural art experiences, which will be my north-star goal for this curriculum. The content I output will demonstrate a microcosm of this vision as a call to build.
